Glacial change detection mapping and analysis in Himachal region using combination of datasets (ASTER, DEM and Drone Imagery).

Project - Department of Science and Technology (WIGH), Dehradun (Jan' - May, 2023)

In a focused endeavor centered on glacial change detection and mapping within the Himachal Region, I delved into the intricate landscape using machine learning (ML) algorithms. Leveraging Python as the programming language of choice, I meticulously implemented these algorithms to analyze the evolving glacial dynamics. The project involved a meticulous comparison of various ML algorithms against ground truth data to ascertain their accuracy, thereby informing decisions regarding their optimal utilization in detecting and mapping glacial changes.

Additionally, I undertook the modeling of the region's Digital Elevation Model (DEM) through a combination of drone imagery and data from the Advanced Spaceborne Thermal Emission and Reflection Radiometer (ASTER). This comprehensive approach not only provided a detailed representation of the terrain but also facilitated a deeper understanding of the spatial patterns of glacial activity. Furthermore, I ventured into the realm of environmental modeling by conducting carbon dioxide concentration mapping of the region.

Analysis of major coastline changes along southern Karnataka coast, India between 1965, 2006 and 2016 using Google Earth Engine.

Project - RRSC (NRSC), Bengaluru (May - july, 2022)

Utilizing advanced geospatial analysis techniques facilitated by Google Earth Engine, the study aims to assess and quantify alterations in coastline morphology spanning five decades. By leveraging the capabilities of Google Earth Engine, I have meticulously analyzed land-water interfaces, identified erosion and accretion patterns, and provided insights into the driving forces behind these changes.

Through the implementation of change detection algorithms, I have effectively identified alterations in coastline positions across the selected time periods. Furthermore, I have quantified rates of erosion and accretion to gain a comprehensive understanding of the magnitude of coastal changes. In addition, I have employed deep learning algorithms to automatically delineate coastlines, enhancing the efficiency and accuracy of the analysis process. This integration of deep learning techniques has contributed to a more robust and comprehensive evaluation of coastline dynamics in the study area.
